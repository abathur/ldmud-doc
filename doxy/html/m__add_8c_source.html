10 Add (or replace) an entry with index <key> in mapping <map>.
11 The modified mapping is also returned as result.
12 
13 The values for the entry are taken from the <data> arguments.
14 Unassigned entry values default to 0, extraneous <data> arguments
15 are ignored.
16 
17 The difference between m_add() and the operator += is that the
18 latter creates a duplicate of the target mapping if it has
19 several references (see EXAMPLES).
