1 master {#driver_master_master}
2 ==============================
3 This directory contains descriptions for the functions that
4 LDMud expects to find in the master object (similar to lfuns,
5 but for the master object only). The name of the master object
6 is hardcoded in the parser, but can also be specified on
7 the commandline (usually to "secure/master").
8 
9 The master is the gateway between the interpreter and the
10 mudlib to perform actions with mudlib specific effects. Calls
11 to the master by the interpreter have an automatic catch() in
12 effect. Functions to be called by the driver can be public
13 or static, but not private.
14 
15 Note that the master is loaded first of all objects. Thus you
16 shouldn't inherit an other object, nor is the compiler able to
17 search include files (read: they must be specified with full
18 path).
19 
20 @par note:you can inherit, but the file will be loaded then before the master, which isn't good for most files.
21 
22 ## SYSTEM STARTUP #
23 
24 The Initialisation functions are called after (re)loading the
25 master to establish the most basic operation parameters.
26 
27 The initialisation of the mud on startup follows this schedule:
28 
29 - The interpreter evaluates the commandline options and
30 initializes itself.
31 - The master is loaded, but since the driverhooks are not set yet,
32 no standard initialisation lfun is called.
33 - get_master_uid() is called. If the result is valid, it becomes the
34 masters uid.
35 - inaugurate_master() is called.
36 - flag() is called for each given '-f' commandline option.
37 - get_simul_efun() is called.
38 - the WIZLIST is read in.
39 - epilog() is called. If it returns an array of strings,
40 they are given one at a time as argument to preload().Traditionally, these strings are the filenames of the objects topre-load, which preload() then does.
41 - The interpreter sets up the IP communication and enters
42 the backend loop.
43 
44 ## RELOADED MASTER OBJECT #
45 
46 If the master is reloaded during system operation, these
47 actions are taken:
48 
49 - The master is loaded, and its initialisation lfun is
50 called according to the settings of the driverhooks (if set).
51 - Any auto-include string and all driverhooks are cleared.
52 - get_master_uid() is called. If the result is valid, it becomes the
53 masters uid and euid.
54 - inaugurate_master() is called.
55 
56 ## DESTRUCTED MASTER OBJECT #
57 
58 If the master was destructed, but couldn't be reloaded, the old
59 master object could be reactivated. In that case:
60 
61 - reactivate_destructed_master() is called.
62 - inaugurate_master() is called.

67

69 called directly from other objects and can be made private or
70 static. Unlike create(), these functions that are applied to
71 the master object are found by the interpreter even if not
