TYPE¶

float

DESCRIPTION¶

A floating point number in the absolute range __FLOAT_MIN__ to __FLOAT_MAX__ (typically 1e-38 to 1e+38). Floating point numbers are signified by a ‘.’ appearing, e.g. ‘1’ is integer 1, but ‘1.’ is floating-point 1 .
